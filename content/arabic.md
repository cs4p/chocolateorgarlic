Monday was my first day of Arabic class at school. Now that we are in the second semester, the people who had art last semester, now have Arabic, which means I have Arabic.

I am very thankful that I was sitting next to a super nice girl who speaks English, because without her I would have been lost.

I only have Arabic once a week for an hour and a half, which is not a lot of time, but I already learned some things in the first class.

Arabic is quite confusing because there are 28 letters in the alphabet, and they can all be written at least three ways.

Luckily, I have a good teacher, and nice classmates to help me out.

I am glad that I get to learn a little bit of Arabic this year! Looking forward to my next Arabic class!

-Naomi
