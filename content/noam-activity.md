Today, instead of a normal Naom activity (youth group), we had a special activity. We went to Emek Refaim (a popular street) to do fun tasks. Some tasks we had were to take selfies with random people, spelling out the name of our Noam age group with our bodies, making a human pyramid, and recreating the friends photo where they are all drinking milkshakes.

After doing tasks for about an hour, we went to the tachana (the tachana is like an outdoor mall) for burgers. Yum! I still can’t get over the amazingness of kosher restaurants everywhere!
