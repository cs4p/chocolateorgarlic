On Friday we went to Rosh Hanikra. While we were driving, we saw seven corn stands right off of the highway.

Even though Ima called ahead to make sure they would be open, when we got there, they were closed. We ended up just sitting on rocks at the beach drawing and listening to music.

After sitting at the beach we went to Nahariya, a small beach town not far away, to grab some food. Despite the heat, we got pizza which was really yummy and we sat by the water to eat it. Then we got yogurt with toppings from a place twenty feet away from where we had been sitting.

The coast of Israel on the Mediterranean Sea is so beautiful and being so close to the water provides a really nice breeze. When I was eating my yogurt, the wind was so strong that it blew the yogurt from my spoon to my leg.

In Israel there are a lot of places that have yogurt and toppings. It is like fro-yo and is super delicious.
