Farewell Toronto!

Whenever I am in Toronto I always make sure to get sushi from sushi on Bloor. The reason this food is always requested is because of the delicious sushi pizza. Sushi pizza is made of crispy rice, onions, tobiko, and special sauce, with a few pieces of fresh salmon sashimi placed on top. If you are ever in Toronto make sure to stop and go to Sushi on Bloor for some incredible sushi pizza.

This dish would not be improved by garlic but it would not taste worse by the addition of garlic.

– Naomi
