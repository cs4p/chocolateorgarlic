About one week ago I had my first sleepover in Israel with my friend Zohar, who is in my class at school. She lives only a block away, but I still got lost (for the second time that day!). Luckily Zohar found me. We got to her house and planned to watch the Descendants trilogy but prime video was not working so we settled on binge watching “fuller house” and “sugar rush”. We had a lot of yummy snacks including olive crackers with hummus.

In the morning we made chocolate chip pancakes and they were delicious.

What a great sleepover!

– Naomi
