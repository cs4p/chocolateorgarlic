Wednesday night, June 17th, we went out to eat at a kosher Brazilian steakhouse.

I had never had Brazilian meat before, and only after eating here, did I realize how much I had been missing. Everything was incredible and filled with so much flavor.

We ordered a Brazilian kabab, a London broil, chicken thighs, chicken legs, rice with a delicious sauce that was called Feijoada, and a dessert.

The kabab was fantastic, and was seasoned so well.

The chicken thighs were amazing! It was probably the best thighs I have ever had. The thighs were so amazing, that we ordered seconds.

The legs were pretty good, but not as incredible as the thighs and the kebab.

The London broil was good, but I liked the other dishes we got better.

My favorites were the kebab, the thighs, and the rice with sauce.

Normally, after we did something, or ate somewhere, Alexandra or I would take notes for when we would post about it, but it seems I forgot to write my thoughts about the dessert we ordered. Although, even without notes, I can only assume that the dessert was delicious.

– Naomi

P.S. Everything would have been improved with garlic, except the dessert, which I can only guess would have been better with chocolate

P.P.S. The restaurant also had an all you can eat meat meal that we had thought would be way too much food for us, but after we got our food, and we learned how delicious everything was, we realized that we should have done the all you can eat meal
