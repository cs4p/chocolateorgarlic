Yesterday we arrived in Budapest. After going to the airbnb and getting some food we headed to a bath house. Hungary is famous for it’s bathhouses and these bathhouses definitely live up to the hype.

In Hungary, bathhouses are party central and are open all hours of the day. The bathhouse we went to was huge! It had both inside and outside parts. There were pools of different temperatures, steam rooms, saunas, and more.

Besides bathhouses, I love all the beautiful architecture. I have seen modern buildings and old historic buildings, and they all are magnificent.
