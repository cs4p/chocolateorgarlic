On Monday, we spent the day in Tel Aviv with Ariana and Sarah. It was so much fun!

Unfortunately, our day started MUCH EARLIER than we planned. Sarah woke up at 1:30 am and Naomi, Ariana, and I woke up at 4:30 am. Our mom, more lucky than us, slept until 7:00. Once we were up, there was no chance of going back to sleep so we talked, played on phones, played 2 games of Catan, and ate pastries from Roladin (a bakery).

Once we got to Tel Aviv, we took a bus to Jaffa. There, we got some food from a bakery that was not very good. Then, we walked quickly around a flee market before making our way to Neve Tzedek where we looked in some stores and got the most delicious yogurt. (Thanks Sara for the recommendation!)

The yogurt was SO good! We all had a mix of acai yogurt and normal with fruit on top. ???? ?

Then, we went to Shuk HaCarmel. In the shuk, we bought elephant pants and bucket hats.

After the shuk, we headed to a hair place where both Noami and Ariana got hair wraps.

Finally we headed to dinner! The restaurant was on the water so the view was gorgeous and the food was great. Our whole dinner was lots of little appetizers that were so good. Of course we also needed dessert, so we got mini crème bruleè that were delicious.
