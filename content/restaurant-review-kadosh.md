On Thursday night, we went to a restaurant, Kadosh, and it was one of the best meals we have had this year.

The restaurant is located near Mamilla and Yafo street so it is very convenient when you are downtown. The restaurant is in a cute bakery with great service and the most delicious desserts ever.

We ordered a salad and two specials plus, of course, a dessert. The specials we got were mushroom soup and gnocchi. The salad and mushroom soup were tasty but the gnocchi was so much better. It was out of this world! I always love gnocchi and this was one extremely delicious. For dessert we got a mousse thing that was so rich and delicious.

I would rate Kadosh 5 stars.
