On Tuesday we went to the Israel Museum. Before going to look at some exhibits, we ate in one of the two restaurants.

I would give Modern (that is the name if the restaurant) 4.5 stars.

At the Israel Musuem there is both a dairy café and a meat restaurant. We went to the meat restaurant and it was delicious. There is a nice atmosphere and good service. I highly recommend eating there if you are every at the Israel Museum.

I ate steak, Naomi had sashimi, and Ima got ground beef in filo dough. We also got a side of beets and for desert almond cigars with parve ice cream and a chocolate mouse with peanut butter also with parve ice cream. All the food was really tasty, especially the almond cigars with ice cream. I would definitely go back for those cigars.
