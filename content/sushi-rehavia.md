Hello! I finally have another food post.

Yesterday I went to Sushi Rehavia. This was the first sushi I’ve had in Israel this year. Definitely an important milestone. We ordered a few dishes and my absolute favorite was the tempura mushrooms. Yum! ?? They have lots of unique rolls. A an array of hits and misses. One hit was the panko salmon roll. And one miss was the rainbow roll.

Sushi Rehavia has multiple locations but we went to the one on Emek Refaim. Emek Refaim is a great place for eating, since there are restaurants and cafes all along the street.

-Alexandra

P.S. Sushi (in my opinion) is not improved from either chocolate or garlic. All though most dishes would still taste okay with garlic, I definitely prefer sushi without garlic.
