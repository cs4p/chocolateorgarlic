At our school we have something called וועדת חודש. That means monthly committee.

Every month four or five girls are responsible for planning fun activities for the class. Usually, the activities include food.

Today, my class watched a movie that the girls in charge of planning for this month organized. Last week after we went to the Binale, the picnic we had was also organized by the monthly committee. When we need to bring food, the girls in charge fill out a sheet saying what girl needs to be bring what food.
