On Monday, June 15th, we went on a hike at Nachal Prat נחל פרת. We had already been previously a few weeks before, and had enjoyed it so much that we decided to come back.

This time (the second time), we brought Alexandra’s and my good friend along.

Nachal Prat is in the desert, but it is a great place to hike because there are lots of pools of water to go into to cool off.

It was not very busy either time we went, and both times it was very hot, so we were glad there was water to go into.

The only problem was that the pools of water had a lot of fish in them. But, once we got in, they mostly swam away from us.

There was a surprising amount of green considering that we were in the desert, in Israel, in June.

We had so much fun both times we went, and the landscape was gorgeous.

I didn’t take a lot of photos, but I will share what I have. Also, the photos are a mix from the two times that we went.

-Naomi
