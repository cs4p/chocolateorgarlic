Last Wednesday, as part of Yom Haatzmaut יום העצמאות there was an air show.

There were four planes and they were flying over all of the hospitals in Israel.

To try and see the brief show, we went up to the roof of our building for the first time in me all year.

In order get to the roof, you have to climb a ladder, open a latch, and go through a whole in the ceiling. The ladder was not exactly sturdy, but we’re all fine.

It was really cool to see parts of Jerusalem from the roof, and fun to explore something new. However, I would have preferred a sturdier way to get up to the roof.

I hope everyone is doing well in isolation/social distancing/ quarantine/ at home.

– Naomi
