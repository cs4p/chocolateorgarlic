Who Knows One is popular pesach song that is sung at the end of the Seder. I, with the help of Alexandra, have come up with a corona version. Here it is below:

13:00 is the time I wake up

12 is the minutes till I go crazy

11 are the letters of coronavirus

10 are the days since I’ve been outside

9 are the shows I watch a day

8 are the stores it takes to find hand sanitizer

7 is half of the days of a quarantine sentence

6 is the feet of social distancing

5 are the weeks since the craziness started

4 are the weeks since I’ve been to school

3 are the minutes of exercise

2 are the gloves on my hands

And 1 is the mask on my face and yours

I hope you enjoy this version of the song! – Naomi
