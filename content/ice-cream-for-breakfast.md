The first Saturday of February is national ice cream for breakfast day.

This morning, a good friend of Alexandra’s and I hosted ice cream for breakfast. They hosted it today, on a Friday, because they wanted more people to be able to come.

There were lots of people there all celebrating ice cream for breakfast day. It was super fun and I ate a lot of delicious ice cream.

Our friend’s family has been hosting ice cream for breakfast for over 20 years and it is a tradition that I would like to keep up in Seattle.

I recommend that all of you practice this tradition too.

Happy ice cream for breakfast day!

-Naomi

P.S. Ice cream would always be improved with chocolate
