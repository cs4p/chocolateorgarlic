Last Thursday we drove from Granada to Marbella.

On our drive to Marbella, we stopped at a town called Nerja. We had a snack, and looked out at the Mediterranean Sea. Alexandra and I ordered Pina Coladas (without alcohol) and they were delicious!

We didn’t do much in Marbella, but we did manage to see flamenco (a kind of dance originally from Spain). We saw flamenco at a small bar meant for tourists, so the show was fine, but not great.

In the audience, there was a little boy (maybe around 3), and at multiple points in the show he got up and danced. It was really cute!

Flamenco dance is nothing like I have seen before. I wouldn’t say that I like it, but I’m glad that I got to see it.

-Naomi
