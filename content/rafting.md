When we were in Toronto, after quarantining for two weeks, we went rafting on the Grand River in Cambridge Ontario. We went with our Uncle Josh and his girlfriend, Emma, and had a great time. Every person sat in their own luxurious inner tube with a backrest and had a kayak paddle. Since there had not been rain in many days, the river was super shallow, in some areas as shallow as 4 inches, and we had to use the paddle anytime we got stuck. The river was also very slow so we used the paddles to move faster. It took us 3 hours from start to finish and it was like a lazy river. It was so relaxing, super fun, and a corona-safe activity.
