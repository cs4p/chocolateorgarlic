Friday morning, as our last activity in Jordan, we went on a Jeep ride.

We were told it would be a Jeep ride, but really we were sitting in the back of a pickup truck on benches that were not the most sturdy.

During the ride, we drove through the desert and saw lots of red rock mountains, it seemed like we were on Mars. We stopped two times, the first to climb a rock mountain, and the second was a sand dune. At the sand dune we walked/ran up (it was actually quite hard because sand makes it harder to move), climbed some rocks, and then ran down the sand dune, which was really fun.

The Jeep ride was really cool, and I enjoyed climbing all of the rocks. It was a great way to end our time in Jordan.

– Naomi
