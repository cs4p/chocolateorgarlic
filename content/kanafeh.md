Yesterday, we tried a new dessert called Kanafeh.

Kanafeh is made from cheese, semolina, crushed pistachio, and a little sugar syrup on top. This treat was one of the best things I have ever eaten.

I ate this sweet in Ramalah, but it was made by someone from Nablus. We were told that Nablus has the best Kanafeh.

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](https://i2.wp.com/chocolateorgarlic.com/wp-content/uploads/img_9328-scaled.jpg?fit=584%2C779&ssl=1)

Maybe it doesn’t look appetizing, but it is. It is so sugary, cheesy, and delicious.

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](https://i1.wp.com/chocolateorgarlic.com/wp-content/uploads/img_9329-scaled.jpg?fit=584%2C779&ssl=1)

We might’ve polished off 4 helpings…

-Alexandra

P.S. Shockingly, this dessert would not be improved with garlic or chocolate. I mean you can’t mess with perfection.
