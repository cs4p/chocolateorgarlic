On Friday, as part of our food tour, we went to a restaurant to try cheese. The owner was a character!!! He said he was the king of Rome and Italy and then the world.

At the restaurant, we tried 3 different cheeses, 1 chocolate, bread with olive tapenade, and bread with extra virgin olive oil.

Did you know that olive oil is really good for your skin? Well it is, so the owner said that he uses an olive oil soap every day to look good. I don’t know how true it is, but he was really funny.
