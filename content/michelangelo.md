Today we saw lots of Michelangelo’s work.

Since we were in Florence, we had to see David. We saw David and other art pieces Michelangelo did that were in the same museum, the Accademia.

When my mom had told me we were going to see David- A HUGE naked man, I was horrified! Why would I want to see a giant naked statue? However, I am very glad we went. David didn’t seem gross, it is really was just fantastic handiwork, great proportions, and so human like.

Besides David, I enjoyed Michelangelo’s Prisoners. There are four prisoners- Awakening Prisoner, Young Prisoner, Bearded Prisoner, and the Atlas Prisoner. These unfinished figures seem to be fighting the stone to free themselves.
