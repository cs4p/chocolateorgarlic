Two nights ago was the best meal I have had yet in Rome (the meal was improved by multiple desserts).

We went to a local pizzeria (local is WAY better that touristy places) that our Papa and aunt had been to in June and had liked. I always trust my aunt with food recommendations because she is a foodie just like Alexandra and I.

We ordered croquettes, a pizza, gnocchi, and an eggplant parmesan. The pizza we ordered had arugula, honey, and walnuts and it was DELICIOUS!!! ? the other dishes we also good, but the pizza was my favorite.

Then, because you can’t have a meal without dessert, we got a classic tiramisu. It tasted like heaven in my mouth! One tiramisu was just not enough so we got another, this time with pistachios. This restaurant is definitely worth going back to.

Tiramisu was just not enough dessert, so we also got our daily gelato from the gelato place we had been trying to find the day before. I got tiramisu and chocolate gelato, staying in theme with the other desserts that night.

-Naomi

P.S. Everything would be improved with garlic except for the tiramisu and gelato, both of which would be improved by chocolate
