On Sunday, we went to the Dead Sea with Cary and Jeron.

I have been to the Dead Sea before but this was probably my favorite time.

Never before have I covered myself in Dead Sea mud which I always wanted to do. The mud is supposed to be good for your skin so I didn’t mind the side effect of orange skin.

Just before leaving, we stopped in the gift shop because of course we needed some Dead Sea products. The guy working there showed us a face mask that is taken away with a magnet! Somehow, because of the Dead Sea minerals, the mask easily comes off with a magnet. Since it was really cool we decided we needed to get it.
