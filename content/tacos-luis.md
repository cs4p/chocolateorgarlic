Tacos Luis, a Mexican restaurant in downtown Jerusalem, has become one of our favorite and most visited restaurants this year.

It is near the shuk, Mamila, and Kadosh (another delicious restaurant) so the location is great if you are coming from anywhere downtown.

Before this year, we had never had meat in our Mexicanfood, so it was a new experience for us that we really enjoyed.

All of the food at Tacos Luis is fabulous!!! You can watch them making the tortillas as you order, which is super fun. Also, they make churros which you can have for dessert.

This is a decently young restaurant, and yet it is very popular.

Another cool thing about the restaurant is that one of Ima’s close friends is cousins with the owners.

I don’t remember exactly what kinds of tacos I have gotten, but all the ones I have tried have all been delicious.

-Naomi
