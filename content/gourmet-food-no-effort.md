There are some frozen kosher delicacies that you can only buy in Israel. We found some great frozen foods, but we also had some big misses.

One of the frozen delicacies we found was fried cauliflower. So yummy and requires no work!

We also have enjoyed soft pretzels, blintzes (which you can also get in the US), pesto cubes, my new favorite popsicles, frozen crepes, and, the best one yet, frozen caramelized onion cubes!

For those people with access to a meat kitchen when they are in Israel, there are endless possibilities of meats and meat products that are only kosher in Israel!

For those of you guys that only buy foods with a hecksher, Israel is the best place to shop! In Israel, so many more foods have heckshers; it is much easier to find something you can buy when shopping.

-Alexandra
