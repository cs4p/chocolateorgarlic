After the thunder and lighting that took place two nights ago and yesterday, it rained for the first real time this year (it had rained a week or two ago, but it had been so light you couldn’t even feel it). ?

I’m so glad it rained!!! Yesterday was the first day since I got to Israel where I wished I was wearing warmer clothes.

I have been missing Seattle’s weather a lot, and yesterday’s rain and wind was just what I needed after the past month and half of incredibly hot weather.

I got the rain I wanted, now I’m just waiting for the snow! ❄️ ⛄️
