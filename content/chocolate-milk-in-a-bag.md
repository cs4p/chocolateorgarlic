One of my favorite Israeli snacks is שוקו בשקית (chocolate milk in a bag). It might sound strange, but it is delicious. ?

It is just chocolate milk in a bag, but for some reason it tastes better than chocolate milk not in a bag. That might just be because it is more fun to drink it in a bag.

When drinking shoko (chocolate milk) you rip off a corner and drink.

I am going to miss drinking shoko when we are back in Seattle. ?

– Naomi

P.S. Chocolate milk would be improved with more chocolate
